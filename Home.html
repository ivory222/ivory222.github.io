BE by Ivory


Chris Ivory Kelechi is a self-taught painter and UI designer, she works with illuminating colors and distinctive pattern, she was born and raised in Nigeria in 2001. A native of Nkwerre, Imo state, she's a conscientious student studying chemical engineering, she has always believed art choose her "it's a compulsive gift I have and I'm not going to fight it anymore but embrace and bolster it". she bases her creative exploration with bright colors on the careful observation of human behavior, detecting semiotic relationship that might be overlooked to an unperceptive viewer. she unmasked the traits, feelings and behavioral pattern we tend to hide, she emphasis on showing people in their most natural state- free. With this approach, the artist constructs situations that invite refection on unspoken matter in today's world. It's more than just art for her but a means to express her creative observations of human's reactions and response through hardship and struggle.
